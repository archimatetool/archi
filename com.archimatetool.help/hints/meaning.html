A meaning is defined as the knowledge or expertise present in the representation of a business object, given a particular context.

It represents the functionality of a representation. It describes the intent of a representation, how it informs the external user.

A certain interpretation of a representation to a certain user carries a certain meaning.

The name of a meaning should preferably be a noun or noun phrase.

Category:

Business. Passive Structure.

Examples:

Policy explanation, Insurance Policy notification.

Typical Relationships:

Association - representation
